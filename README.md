# Sandbox for RA Assessment

### Overview:
This assessment simulates a typical Research Assistant task within our NUMI project development workflow. You'll be performing quality assurance on exercise content to ensure it meets our standards before deployment.

### Context:  
Our NUMI research project follows a structured content development process for each exercise.  
Specifically - Scrape Master Question > QA Master (first-pass) > QA Master (on NUMI) > Populate Variations > QA Variations. Ideally, with enough attention to detail on the master, the QA for the 49 variations will not be tedious.

Your role focuses on the **QA Master (first-pass)** stage, which is critical for ensuring variation quality downstream. Thorough attention to detail at this stage prevents tedious QA work on the 49 variations that follow.

### Task Requirements: 
You will quality-check web-scraped content to identify and correct:
* Scraping errors and mistakes
* Typos and formatting issues
* Missing or incorrect information

This assessment evaluates your attention to detail, organizational skills, and ability to collaborate effectively on research projects.

### Quality Assurance Checklist:
For QA Master (first-pass), I've outlined what to look out for for each part of the exercise:
* Question text: 
    * **Missing content**: Check for incomplete words, phrases, or mathematical expressions
    * **Typos**: Verify proper formatting, especially fractions (must use \\frac{x}{y} format)
    * **Coherence**: Ensure questions are logical and clearly understood
    * **Images (if available)**: Check images match question text
* ⁠Correct answers: Look out for
    * **Accuracy**: Verify answers match the question requirements
    * **Alternatives**: Identify and note any additional correct answers on the sheet
* Solutions: Look out for
    * **Standalone quality**: Solutions should be complete and self-contained
    * **Clarity**: Note if solutions need rewriting for better understanding
    * **Step structure**: Evaluate if steps should be broken down further or combined
    * **Hint effectiveness**: Ensure first two steps work well as NUMI tutoring hints
    * **Completeness**: Check for missing information, words, or typos
    * **Images (if available)**: Check images match solution text
 
### Success Criteria
* Systematic review of all assigned questions
* Accurate identification and correction of errors
* Clear documentation of findings and recommendations
* Organized collaboration through proper sheet updates
